Farming Guide Location

Farming materials is an important aspect of Genshin Impact gameplay, as it allows players to obtain resources needed for character leveling, weapon upgrading, cooking, and other activities. There are various types of materials that can be farmed, including character ascension materials, talent upgrade materials, weapon ascension materials, and specialty items.
