Best Weapons

Staff of Homa

CRIT DMG (66.2%)

HP increased by 20~40%. Additionally, provides an ATK Bonus based on 0.8~1.6% of the wielder's Max HP. When the wielder's HP is less than 50%, this ATK bonus is increased by an additional 1~1.8% of Max HP.

Dragon's Bane

Elemental Mastery (221)

Increases DMG against opponents affected by Hydro or Pyro by 20~36%.

Weapon Substitute

Primordial Jade Spear

CRIT Rate (22.1%)

On hit, increases ATK by 3.2~6.0% for 6s. Max 7 stacks. This effect can only occur once every 0.3s. While in possession of the maximum possible stacks, DMG dealt is increased by 12~24%.

Staff of the Scarlet Sands

CRIT Rate (44.1%)

The equipping character gains 52~104% of their Elemental Mastery as bonus ATK. When an Elemental Skill hits opponents, the Dream of the Scarlet Sands effect will be gained for 10s: The equipping character will gain 28~56% of their Elemental Mastery as bonus ATK. Max 3 stacks.

Deathmatch

CRIT Rate (36.8%)

If there are at least 2 opponents nearby, ATK is increased by 16~32% and DEF is increased by 16~32%. If there are fewer than 2 opponents nearby, ATK is increased by 24~48%.

The Catch

Energy Recharge (45.9%)

Increases Elemental Burst DMG by 16~32% and Elemental Burst CRIT Rate by 6~12%.

Wavebreaker's Fin

ATK (13.8%)

For every point of the entire party's combined maximum Energy capacity, the Elemental Burst DMG of the character equipping this weapon is increased by 0.12~0.24%. A maximum of 40~80% increased Elemental Burst DMG can be achieved this way.

Talent Priority

Elemental Burst

Rites of Termination

Rosaria's unique take on this prayer ritual: First, she swings her weapon to slash surrounding opponents; then, she summons a frigid Ice Lance that strikes the ground. Both actions deal Cryo DMG. While active, the Ice Lance periodically releases a blast of cold air, dealing Cryo DMG to surrounding opponents.

Elemental Skill

Ravaging Confession

Rosaria swiftly shifts her position to appear behind her opponent, then stabs and slashes them with her polearm, dealing Cryo DMG. This ability cannot be used to travel behind opponents of a larger build.

Normal Attack

Spear of the Church

Normal Attack
Performs up to 5 consecutive spear strikes.

Charged Attack
Consumes a certain amount of Stamina to lunge forward, dealing damage to opponents along the way.

Plunging Attack
Plunges from mid-air to strike the ground below, damaging opponents along the path and dealing AoE DMG upon impact.

Best 4 Piece Set Artifact

Noblesse Oblige

4-Piece Set

Using an Elemental Burst increases all party members' ATK by 20% for 12s. This effect cannot stack.

Gilded Dreams

4-Piece Set

Within 8s of triggering an Elemental Reaction, the character equipping this will obtain buffs based on the Elemental Type of the other party members, ATK is increased by 14% for each party member whose Elemental Type is the same as the equipping character, and Elemental Mastery is increased by 50 for every party member with a different Elemental Type. Each of the aforementioned buffs will count up to 3 characters. This effect can be triggered once every 8s. The character who equips this can still trigger its effects when not on the field.

Lavawalker

4-Piece Set

Increases DMG against opponents affected by Pyro by 35%.
