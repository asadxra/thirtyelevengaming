Best 5 Star Options

When choosing five-star weapons, consider your character's playstyle, elemental abilities, and weapon preferences. Look for weapons that synergize well with your character's abilities and aim to maximize their damage potential. Additionally, consider the role and elemental affinity of your character to make the most effective weapon choices.

Redhorn Stonethresher

CRIT DMG (88.2%)

DEF is increased by 28~56%. Normal and Charged Attack DMG is increased by 40~80% of DEF.

Skyward Pride

Energy Recharge (36.8%)

Increases all DMG by 8~16%. After using an Elemental Burst, Normal or Charged Attack, on hit, creates a vacuum blade that does 80~160% of ATK as DMG to opponents along its path. Lasts for 20s or 8 vacuum blades.

Best 4 Star Options

While five-star weapons are highly desirable, the game's content can still be completed and enjoyed with four-star weapons. They can serve as valuable alternatives until you obtain the desired five-star weapon. Additionally, refining and upgrading your weapons using enhancement materials can further increase their power and effectiveness. The following weapons are most viable for this build.

Serpent Spine

CRIT Rate (27.6%)

Every 4s a character is on the field, they will deal 6~10% more DMG and take 3~2% more DMG. This effect has a maximum of 5 stacks and will not be reset if the character leaves the field, but will be reduced by 1 stack when the character takes DMG.

Whiteblind

DEF (51.7%)

On hit, Normal or Charged Attacks increase ATK and DEF by 6~12% for 6s. Max 4 stacks (24~48% total). Can only occur once every 0.5s.

Favonius Greatsword

Energy Recharge (61.3%)

CRIT hits have a 60~100% chance to generate a small amount of Elemental Particles, which will regenerate 6 Energy for the character. Can only occur once every 12~6s.

Team Ideas

Having the right team is crucial for success in various challenges and domains.
